4

Connect & flash

Plug in your XIAO ESP32-C3 via USB-C. Hold the BOOT button while pressing RESET to enter download mode (the LED will be off).

😴 Pod goes to sleep before you can flash?

The pod firmware deep-sleeps after ~2 seconds. To flash it anyway:

  1. Plug in the pod and immediately press RESET — this gives you a fresh boot window.
  2. Click Erase Flash above and connect the port within that 2-second window.
    (Erasing wipes the firmware so it can't sleep anymore.)
  3. Once erase is done, click Connect & Flash normally — no BOOT/RESET needed.

If the erase window is too tight, hold BOOT while pressing RESET to lock it in download mode before clicking anything.

5

Manual flash (fallback)

If Web Serial doesn't work, download the firmware and flash it with esptool from your terminal.

Troubleshooting: If your browser doesn't see the device, make sure you've installed the CH340 or CP210x USB driver for your operating system. Linux users may need to add their user to the dialout group.
