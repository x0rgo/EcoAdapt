Flash your EcoAdapt Bridge

Auto-configure your XIAO ESP32-C3 in 60 seconds
⚠️ Web Serial requires Chrome, Edge, or Opera on desktop. Safari and Firefox don't support it. Scroll down for the manual download option.

Choose firmware

Pick which device you're flashing right now.

2

Account & server

This is baked into the firmware so the bridge knows where to send data.

3

WiFi (optional)

Pre-stage WiFi so your bridge auto-connects on first boot. Skip this and you can configure WiFi later via Bluetooth or the bridge's setup hotspot.

💡 Tip: For a science fair demo, name your phone hotspot something easy to remember and pre-stage it here. The bridge will reconnect every time you turn the hotspot on.
4

Connect & flash

Plug in your XIAO ESP32-C3 via USB-C. Hold the BOOT button while pressing RESET to enter download mode (the LED will be off).

5

Manual flash (fallback)

If Web Serial doesn't work, download the firmware and flash it with esptool from your terminal.

Troubleshooting: If your browser doesn't see the device, make sure you've installed the CH340 or CP210x USB driver for your operating system. Linux users may need to add their user to the dialout group.
